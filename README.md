## 🌍 About BioHelixNode

**BioHelixNode Research Laboratory** is a biotechnology-focused research and training initiative dedicated to promoting:

- 🧬 Bioinformatics  
- 🧫 Genomics  
- 💻 Computational Biology  
- 🔬 Biotechnology  
- 📊 Biological Data Analysis  
- 🌱 Scientific Capacity Building in Liberia  

Our mission is to create accessible opportunities for scientific learning, collaboration, research, and innovation while supporting the development of future Liberian scientists and researchers.

---

## 🚀 Current Program

### Summer Bioinformatics Internship 2026

An **8-week beginner-friendly online internship** introducing Liberian life sciences students and graduates to bioinformatics, computational biology, genomics, and biological data analysis.
